Beyond the Smile: Capturing Micro Expressions with Event-based Vision

The semester project titled "Beyond the Smile: Capturing Micro Expressions with Event-based Vision" explores the utilization of event-based vision systems to detect and analyze micro-expressions. Leveraging high temporal precision of event cameras, the project integrates these with advanced machine learning algorithms to enhance the accuracy and speed of micro-expression recognition. The findings suggest significant potential for applications in fields like psychology, security, and human-computer interaction, offering a deeper understanding of human emotions beyond traditional methods.
